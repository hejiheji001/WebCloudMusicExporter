üòÜ Introduction

This is a Netease Music analyser which can help you find out the true address of music(320K preferred). Even copyrighted

There are 3 working modes, default mode(simply press Enter) is "Go Auto" which detects your input and try to give correct result

If you know what your input type exactly, you can choose one of another 2 modes hidden in the triangle

üòú Usage

1. Find a song or a playlist in Netease Music website

2. Copy the link or ID and paste it into the box below

3. Press Enter on your keyboard or select a specific function.

4. Download or push the results into your Baidu Yun.

üò≥ Notice

Inputs

Links similar to "http://music.163.com/song?id=29572501" refer to a SONG and its Music ID is 29572501 HINT: Keyword is "song"

Links similar to "http://music.163.com/#/playlist?id=41370921" refer to a PLAYLIST and its Playlist ID is 41370921 HINT: Keyword is "playlist"

Links from desktop devices are recommended. The ones from mobile devices are OK but the auto mode may be malfunction

Tricks

Songs' quality in playlist is better than not-in-playlist ones. So it's wise to create a new playlist contains all your needed songs

You can export the download list by click the Export button. Support aria2c, curl and wget

Troubleshooting

If you are located in China mainland, you can simply download songs directly. Otherwise please use a Chinese proxy or push the songs into your Baidu Yun.

Save To Pan button maybe useless and no notifications will given even it works well. So PLEASE check your Baidu Pan manually

If Save To Pan doesn't work, you can try the Chrome extension Baidu Yun Pan which provides a higher reliability
